FEATURED TRIPS

Family Fun Float

Perfect for families and first-timers, this gentle float trip offers stunning scenery and mild rapids. Enjoy a relaxing day on the water with knowledgeable guides and plenty of opportunities for swimming and wildlife spotting.

  • Duration: Half-Day
  • Difficulty: Easy (Class I-II rapids)
  • Minimum Age: 6 years
  • Highlights: Scenic views, calm waters, family-friendly atmosphere

Intermediate Rapids Adventure

Ready for more excitement? This trip features exhilarating Class III rapids, offering a perfect balance of challenge and fun. Our experienced guides will navigate you through thrilling sections while ensuring your safety and enjoyment.

  • Duration: Full-Day
  • Difficulty: Moderate (Class III rapids)
  • Minimum Age: 12 years
  • Highlights: Exhilarating rapids, beautiful canyons, riverside lunch

Advanced Whitewater Challenge

For the ultimate adrenaline seekers, this trip tackles intense Class IV-V rapids. Prepare for a high-octane adventure that will test your limits and leave you with an unforgettable sense of accomplishment. Previous rafting experience recommended.

  • Duration: Multi-Day (2-3 days)
  • Difficulty: Challenging (Class IV-V rapids)
  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Highlights: Big waves, technical maneuvers, wilderness camping
